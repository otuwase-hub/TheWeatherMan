precipitation format:
dd/mm/yyyy city,province low_temperature(C) high_temperature(C) type amount
type {rain, snow, freezing rain, hail, fog}
precipitation example:
25/03/2023 Halifax,NS -1 2 rain 37

wind format:
dd/mm/yyyy city,province low_temperature(C) high_temperature(C) speed(km/hr) direction(degrees)
wind example:
25/03/2023 Halifax,NS -1 2 19 126

sea_state format:
dd/mm/yyyy city,province low_temperature(C) high_temperature(C) height(m) period water_temp(C)
sea_state example:
25/03/2023 Halifax,NS -1 2 0.5 2 7
